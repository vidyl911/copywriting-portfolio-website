Campaign Workflow

Structured funnel for digital educators & creators

1. Instagram Traffic

Organic reels & paid ads to generate interest and engagement, prompting comments or DMs.

⬇

2. Comment / DM Trigger

Automated DM starts conversation with interested leads while momentum is high.

⬇

3. DM Automation & Email Capture

Use ManyChat to qualify leads and collect emails in exchange for a high-value free resource.

⬇

4. Resource Delivery

Send the promised PDF, mini-course, or guide via DM & email to move the lead to owned media.

⬇

5. Email Nurture Sequence

Email 1: Welcome & Pain Point

Introduce yourself, deliver the free resource, and highlight the lead’s key problem.

→

Email 2: Empathy & Promise

Show understanding of their struggles and promise a transformation if they follow the system.

→

Email 3: Client Success Story

Share a case study or testimonial to prove results and build credibility.

→

Email 4: Direct Value Offer

Provide actionable tips, frameworks, or strategies to demonstrate your expertise.

→

Email 5: Direct Pitch

Present your course or mentorship program clearly and invite the lead to purchase or book a call.

⬇

6. Landing Page / Call Booking

Send the lead to a high-converting landing page or Calendly link for strategy calls or course purchase.

⬇

7. Enrollment & Payment

Lead completes purchase and gains access to course or mentorship program.

⬇

8. Onboarding & Activation

Deliver login info, guide first steps, and ensure the client experiences quick wins in your program.
