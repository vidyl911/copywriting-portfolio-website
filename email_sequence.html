5-Part Email Sequence Sample

A sample email sequence for a coaching/mentorship program designed to nurture new leads and convert them into clients.

Email 1 Email 2 Email 3 Email 4 Email 5

Email 1: The Welcome & Pain Point

Subject: A quick note about [Their Goal]…

Hi [First Name],

I’m so glad you subscribed. Thank you for inviting me into your inbox.

Over the years, I've seen so many people like you struggle with the same things: [mention 1-2 key pain points, e.g., feeling stuck in their career, not knowing how to scale their business, or a lack of direction].

I created my program to help people just like you break through those barriers and achieve their goals.

To get us started, I’ve put together a free guide on [Topic of guide, e.g., "The 3 Biggest Mistakes Business Owners Make"]. It's the first step toward getting the clarity you need.

Download My Free Guide

I can't wait to share more with you!

Best,
[Your Name]

Email 2: The Empathy & Promise

Subject: Is this how you feel?

Hey [First Name],

I want to talk to you about something personal.

I know what it’s like to feel [reiterate a key pain point, e.g., "overwhelmed by tasks and a lack of focus"]. You feel like you're putting in the work, but you're not seeing the results. You're trying to do everything yourself, and it's exhausting.

But what if you didn't have to do it alone? What if you had a clear roadmap, a proven system, and a mentor to guide you every step of the way?

That's the promise of my [Your Program Name] program. It's not just another course; it's a partnership designed to help you [mention a key benefit, e.g., "turn your vision into reality" or "unlock your full potential"].

Keep an eye out for my next email, where I'll share a real-world success story from one of my clients.

Cheers,
[Your Name]

Email 3: The Client Success Story

Subject: How [Client Name] went from [Problem] to [Result]

Hi [First Name],

I want to share the story of my client, [Client Name].

When [Client Name] first came to me, they were [briefly describe their "before" state, e.g., "struggling to grow their sales past a certain point"]. They were frustrated, overworked, and unsure of what to do next.

Over the next few months, we worked together to [briefly describe your process, e.g., "develop a custom growth strategy and streamline their operations"].

The result? [Client Name] not only [mention a specific result, e.g., "doubled their revenue"] but also found [mention a non-monetary benefit, e.g., "the clarity and freedom they'd been looking for"].

This is what's possible when you have the right strategy and support.

See the Full Success Story

Talk soon,
[Your Name]

Email 4: The Direct Value Offer

Subject: Here's my "secret" to [Their Goal]

Hi [First Name],

I've been in the mentorship space for years, and I've seen the same questions and roadblocks come up again and again. So I put together a simple, powerful resource that addresses the biggest ones.

I'm giving you my [Name of Your Resource, e.g., "The 5-Step Checklist to Scale Your Business"] completely free. This isn't just theory; it’s the exact framework I use with my own clients to get real results.

Download My Free Resource

If this resource resonates with you and you're ready to get serious about reaching your goals, let's talk.

Book a Call

Best,
[Your Name]

Email 5: The Direct Pitch

Subject: The final step to [Achieving Their Goal]

Hi [First Name],

If you're still reading, it's because you're serious about [achieving their main goal, e.g., "growing your business," "finding your ideal career," or "making a change"].

Over the past few days, I've shared some of my best insights, client success stories, and a free resource. But nothing replaces one-on-one, customized mentorship.

My [Your Program Name] program is designed to get you from where you are to where you want to be as efficiently as possible. It includes:

  • [Benefit 1, e.g., Weekly 1-on-1 coaching calls]
  • [Benefit 2, e.g., A custom roadmap for your business]
  • [Benefit 3, e.g., Direct access to me for questions]

This is the fastest way to get results without the guesswork.

The next step is simple. Let's schedule a call to see if my program is the right fit for you. There's no obligation, just a conversation to help you find the best path forward.

Book My Free Consultation

I look forward to hearing from you.

Best,
[Your Name]
